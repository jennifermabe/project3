Volunteer

BeeFriendly.org is a 501(c)(3) non-profit group based in Irvine,CA that supports educational initiatives, community active postion and mass media engagement to protect and support honey bees of Orange County in California.

We will be glad to meet as a volunteer for our organization to help assisting in the bee gardens, organizing the events, teaching people about the bees importance and the impact of their wellness to the health of our planet.

We are a family that will value each and every help! And as a family we like giving presents to our members.
