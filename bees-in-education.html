WHY DO WE NEED EDUCATION ABOUT BEES?

While it is well known that bees produce honey, many other food sources also rely on bees. Conservative figures show that bees pollinate at least ONE mouthful in every THREE that we eat! Bees around the world, including in Orange County, are in serious trouble. Scientists are seeing a large decline in bee populations worldwide. This poses a threat not only for our food production but our environment as a whole. Education of a society is one of the most important things on the path of saving bees and our life on our planet!
